Company Profile

QI LE ER TOY INDUSTRY CO., LTD. -- Founded in 1997 and located in Shantou, Guangdong, China, our company benefits from a strategic location and a well-established global trade network. Certified by BSCI and ISO9001, we adhere to stringent international standards for quality management and social responsibility. Our 20,000-square-meter production facility is equipped with 20 advanced production lines and supported by a dedicated team of 200 skilled professionals. We integrate product design, R&D, manufacturing, and sales into a comprehensive service chain, with a strong emphasis on innovation and originality.


Our independently developed products hold both domestic and international patents. To meet diverse market demands, our products comply with multiple authoritative certifications—including CE, ROHS, HR4040, UKCA (Europe), and ASTM, CPC, CPSIA (USA)—ensuring full adherence to regional safety and quality regulations.
